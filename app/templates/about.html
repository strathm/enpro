Who We Are

We specialise in the distribution of premium safety workwear and protective accessories, sourced exclusively from trusted, certified manufacturers such as Mascot International and Petzl International. Our extensive product range is designed to meet the diverse needs of various industrial sectors, delivering exceptional protection, long-lasting durability, and all-day comfort, ensuring your workforce stays safe, confident, and ready for any challenge.

Our team provides expert guidance in selecting the most suitable protective workwear and accessories tailored to your specific industry requirements. Whether you're launching a new operation or enhancing existing safety standards, we deliver customised recommendations to ensure compliance and optimal protection for your workforce.

We support companies across Africa with streamlined export solutions, managing everything from documentation to shipping logistics. Our experienced team ensures your safety gear reaches its destination efficiently and in full compliance with both international and local regulations.

Enpro Limited handles the complete customs clearance process, offering a seamless, stress-free experience for our clients across Kenya. We prioritize swift processing and coordinate prompt, reliable delivery directly to your location.

Why Choose Enpro Limited?

Expertise Built Over Nearly a Decade

With close to ten years of hands-on experience in the safety wear and logistics industry, Enpro Limited has developed deep expertise and a reliable network that ensures clients receive tailored, professional service and goods every time.

Certified Products from Global Manufacturers

Our protective workwear and accessories are sourced from globally recognized manufacturers—Mascot International and Petzl International. All our products are rigorously tested and certified to meet or exceed international and industry-specific safety standards.

Seamless Export Support Across Africa

We don’t just supply—we support. By handling export procedures for our clients, we reduce the risks and challenges associated with individual exporting. Our structured approach ensures all documentation and compliance steps are managed effectively, giving our clients peace of mind.

Full Customs Clearance & Efficient Doorstep Delivery

For our local Kenyan clients, Enpro Limited takes care of the entire clearance process, ensuring swift movement through customs and timely delivery directly to our clients’ locations.

Tailored Consultations for Industry-Specific Safety Needs

We understand that every industry has unique safety requirements. That’s why we offer personalized consultations to help clients choose the right protective gear that ensures compliance, comfort, and protection in their specific environments.
